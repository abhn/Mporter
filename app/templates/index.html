Mentors Are Important

And for the right mentorship to happen, it is essential that the mentor knows what the mentee is up to and provide feedback. Mporter aims at reducing the mental friction of having to manually spend time to curate the tasks done, putting them down in an email/message and sending them at a particular interval of the day.

Why Mporter?

  • Free and open source software
  • Private, secure and 100% customisable
  • Easy to use, get started in minutes
  • Customize various parameters of reporting
  • User management system for multi-mentee use
